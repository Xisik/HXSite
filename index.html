Why Choose HXPainter?

Lightning Fast

Experience silky-smooth brush strokes with our OpenGL-backed canvas. No lag, even on large documents.

Professional Tools

Advanced layer management, customizable brushes, and a full suite of editing tools at your fingertips.

Truly Free

Open source and released under GPL v3. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, just pure creativity.

Built from the Real HXPainter App

Desktop Painting Workspace

Work with a menu bar, top toolbar, left tool palette, OpenGL-backed canvas, and right-side docks for layers, history, color, navigator, and performance.

Layer-Local Editing

Brush, erase, fill, select, shape, and text tools operate through the active document and layer system, with opacity, visibility, lock, and merge controls.

Project-Safe Output

Save and load native .hxp projects, import images as layers, export PNG or JPG artwork, and step through edits with command-based Undo and Redo history.
