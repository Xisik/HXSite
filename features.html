Powerful Features for Artists

OpenGL-Backed Canvas

Hardware-accelerated rendering ensures real-time feedback and high FPS even with complex brush settings.

Advanced Layer System

Add, duplicate, merge, and organize layers. Full control over opacity and visibility for professional workflows.

Comprehensive Toolset

Brush, Eraser, Fill, Color Picker, Selection, Shape, and Text tools. Everything you need to bring your vision to life.

Flexible Export

Save your progress in our native .hxp format or export your final masterpiece to high-quality PNG or JPG.

Command-Based Undo/Redo

Mistakes happen. Our robust history system allows you to backtrack and explore different creative paths without worry.

Customizable Interface

A sleek dark theme with dockable panels for Tool Options, Layers, History, and more, allowing you to tailor your workspace.

Engineered as a Native Desktop App

HXPainter is built in C++20 with Qt Widgets, OpenGLWidgets, OpenGL, and SVG resources. The public source repository includes dedicated modules for tools, brush engines, project serialization, export, tablet input mapping, Windows deployment, and GPL-3.0 licensing.
